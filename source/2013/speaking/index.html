Sass Conf

October 2-4, 2014

Share. Speak.

We know you've got some radical thoughts and ideas, you use Sass. Consider sharing them at Sass Conf. Tell us what you've been thinking. Show us what you've been hacking up at work or after hours. Submissions close May 30th.

If you're selected as a speaker

All speakers get a free ticket to the conference, including accommodation for the nights of the conference, flight cost, transportation to and from the airport, all meals, and all the other good stuff.

If you've already bought a ticket, we will give you a full refund if you're selected as a speaker.

Speakers are welcome to bring their partner to the conference, but they need to buy a Partner ticket.

Setting the stage

All perspectives

Sass is but one of many tools we use in our craft. Consider how your presentation is relevant to the Sass community, but don't feel constrained to talking only about Sass or programming/tech.

Fresh ideas and faces

We highly encourage new talks, radical new ideas and approaches. Be passionate about your chosen topic. We especially welcome first-time speakers and speakers from other disciplines.

Workshops

Beginner to advanced levels. Choose from 2 or 4 hour workshop sessions. Workshops should be interactive and hands-on. Your session doesn't have to just be about Sass. Ideally, Sass is one of the tools in your arsenal. We'd love to see topics from learning new tools to implementation to process ideas for small to enterprise teams.

Talks

45-minute talks designed to allow you to dive into your topic. Take us on a great journey through your process or the philosophy behind your designs. Share your stories, make us feel. We're looking for talks that inspire and get us thinking in different ways or open our minds to new solutions and possibilities.

All sessions will be recorded and made available to the public. Catch-up with last year's phenomenal speakers.

Go for it. Applications are still open.

Submit your proposal(s) online.
